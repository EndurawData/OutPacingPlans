🤓 Enduraw advices:

  • 🚩 Do not try new nutrition on race day! Make sure you eat food you know and trained with 🏃‍➡️
  • To counterbalance sweating, make sure you have isotonic drinks or salty food during your effort 🧂
  • Alternate between hot and cold food ☕🧋
  • Chose different carbs inputs to keep taste and motivation 🥨🍫🍑
  • Performance also stand in your pleasure! Add pleasure aliments, and not only carbs if needed 🧀🥚
